Greenhouse Automation - Monitoring

Monitoring Overview

The monitoring system in our greenhouse automation project provides real-time data on various environmental factors to ensure optimal growing conditions for plants. This system allows you to keep track of critical metrics such as temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and light intensity, enabling efficient and informed decision-making.

Key Monitoring Features

  • Real-time tracking of temperature and humidity levels.
  • Soil moisture sensors to monitor and maintain optimal water levels.
  • Light intensity monitoring to ensure proper photosynthesis.
  • Data logging for trend analysis and historical data review.
  • Alerts and notifications for abnormal conditions.

Benefits of Monitoring

Implementing a comprehensive monitoring system in your greenhouse provides several benefits:

  • Improved plant health through constant environmental monitoring.
  • Early detection of potential issues, reducing crop loss.
  • Better resource management, including water and energy usage.
  • Increased crop yield and quality by maintaining optimal growing conditions.
  • Data-driven insights for long-term planning and optimization.
